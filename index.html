2. Enter Your Registered Number

Make sure the number you enter is linked to your Easypaisa account. Wrong numbers may delay your payment.

3. Select Amount

Enter the amount you want to withdraw. Check the minimum withdrawal limit before confirming.

4. Confirm with OTP

You will receive a one-time password. Enter it to confirm your request.

Your money usually arrives within a few hours. After that, you can withdraw cash from any Easypaisa agent or transfer it to your bank.
